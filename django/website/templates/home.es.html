/// Beta Testing

If your internet connection seems slow or if anything weird starts happening with your Internet connection, try taking out the Roxy proxy setting to see if that solves your problem. If so, please report the problem ASAP using the form below or by emailing Ericka ericka@roxyproxy.org. It helps to know the time when a problem occurred.
